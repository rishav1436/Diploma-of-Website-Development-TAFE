Our History Aussie Kidz Charity was founded in 2000 by David Dahl and Ross Tisch. Both men were previously Directors of another children’s charity and were tired of seeing funds increasingly spent on overheads and not the children in need. They set up Aussie Kidz Charity.

Based in Brisbane Australia the Aussie Kids charity seeks to assist disabled and under priveledged kids across Australia. 100% of funds raised go directly to the children to help them with their needs.

To date, Aussie Kidz has raised approximately $900 000 and has distributed these funds by way of 150 grants to kids in need. These funds have been used to purchase equipment and services for disabled and under- privileged children from all over Australia.

Our organisation has the following values:
Integrity – We operate with professionalism and honesty in everything we do.
Accountability – We remain volunteer-based with 100% of funds raised going to support the kids. We are committed to running a lean and efficient charity.
Compassion – We have the utmost empathy and respect for our clients and their families and supporters.

If you are passionate about helping kids with a disability live better lives, or like us, want to see 100% of funds raised go directly to such kids, then we’d love you to get involved with Aussie Kidz Charity.

One of the simplest ways to support our work is to donate money to us. All donations $2 and over are tax deductible as we are a DGR (Deductible Gift Recipient) charity.
